About Madhav Seva Nyash Mahakal Mandir

It was established near Shri Mahakaleshwar Temple on the holy land of Ujjaini by preserving many characteristics of Indian culture and Sanatan Dharma. In which the thinking of “Vasuvaidh Kutumbakam” and “Sarvebhavantu Sukhinah” is clearly visible. Since the laying of the foundation stone of the Trust, many such works have been done till date which highlight the feeling of welfare of the entire society.

Out of the 13 bighas of land given by Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh to Madhav Seva Trust, the Trust has donated a large part of this land to Shri Mahakaleshwar Temple Management Committee for the convenience of the devotees, which is being used for the convenience of the visitors.

On the remaining land, so that the devotees coming from all over the country can get all the facilities to stay near the Mahakal Temple and they can leave this city with a feeling of satisfaction, keeping this in mind, a well-equipped Madhav Seva Nyash Mahakal Mandir was constructed by the Trust.
